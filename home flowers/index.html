Interesting Fact

Echeveria - The plant will grow exclusively in nutritious loamy soil with sand admixtures. Without an excessive supply of moisture, it will not survive, but at the same time, stagnation of water will lead to the death of the flower. It is interesting that hairy species of echeveria should be watered carefully, not as often as naked ones, and trying not to get drops on the body of the plant.
